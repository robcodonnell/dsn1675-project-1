ABOUT

I’m a 19 year old painter, typographer and graphic designer, born in Ottawa and attending Algonquin College. Growing up in Ottawa has given me big dreams and taught me as an artist to break the mould and always push the creativity as far as possible, and then a little more. I’m always searching for new styles, and new ways to bring back old styles, and I believe innovation and creativity are necessary for success in design. My background as an artist is primarily in hand lettering, and my style is influenced by calligraphy and graffiti / street art. I do commissions, so check below for information on how to get in contact.
